Each indicator captures a different observable feature of personalist rule. They are grouped into two dimensions: Power Concentration and Personality Cult.

Two-parameter logistic (2PL) IRT model estimated via marginal maximum likelihood (Bock–Aitkin EM). The latent trait θ represents personalism as measured by the three Wikidata indicators.
